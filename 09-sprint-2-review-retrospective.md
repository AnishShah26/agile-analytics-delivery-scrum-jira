Prepare the SaaS datasets, validate data quality and complete churn analysis to identify key customer risk segments.

## Sprint Review

During Sprint 2, the SaaS datasets were reviewed and prepared for analysis. The project considered customer, subscription, usage, support and churn-related data to support churn measurement and segment-level analysis.

Data quality checks focused on reviewing missing values, duplicates, data types and assumptions before using the data for dashboard metrics.

The sprint also completed churn analysis, including the overall churn rate and churn breakdowns by customer segment. This helped identify higher-risk groups for later dashboard reporting and business recommendations.

## Sprint Outcome

Sprint 2 was completed successfully. All 4 planned stories were moved to Done in Jira, completing 11 out of 11 planned story points.

## What Went Well

- The data preparation scope was clearly linked to the dashboard objective.
- Churn analysis was structured around business-relevant customer segments.
- Data validation was included before dashboard development.
- The sprint created a strong analytical foundation for revenue-risk analysis.

## What Could Be Improved

- Future projects should include more detailed data dictionaries.
- Data quality assumptions should be documented earlier in the process.
- Churn definitions should be confirmed with stakeholders before final reporting.

## Lessons Learned

This sprint showed that dashboard quality depends on clear data preparation and metric validation. Before building visuals, the underlying churn logic and customer segmentation need to be reliable and easy to explain.
