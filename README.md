# Agile Analytics Delivery Project | Scrum, Jira & Business Analysis

## Project Overview

This is a simulated Agile delivery case study showing how a SaaS customer churn and revenue-at-risk dashboard can be planned and managed using Scrum, Jira and business analysis artefacts.

The project demonstrates how a Business Analyst or Agile Delivery Analyst can translate a business problem into stakeholder requirements, user stories, acceptance criteria, sprint plans and delivery documentation.

## Business Problem

A SaaS business needs to understand customer churn, identify high-risk customer segments and quantify recurring revenue exposure.

The dashboard is designed to support decision-making for Customer Success, Revenue Operations, Product and Executive stakeholders.
