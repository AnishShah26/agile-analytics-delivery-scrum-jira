# Final Project Summary

## Project Title

Agile Analytics Delivery Project: SaaS Churn Dashboard

## Project Type

Simulated Agile delivery case study

## Project Goal

To plan and manage the Agile delivery of a SaaS customer churn and revenue-at-risk dashboard using Scrum, Jira and business analysis artefacts.

## Business Problem

A SaaS business needs to understand customer churn, identify high-risk customer segments and quantify recurring revenue exposure. The goal of the dashboard is to help Customer Success, Revenue Operations, Product and Executive stakeholders make clearer retention decisions.

## My Role

Business Analyst / Agile Delivery Analyst

## Key Responsibilities

- Defined project scope and stakeholder requirements.
- Created stakeholder requirements and dashboard success criteria.
- Structured the work into epics, user stories and acceptance criteria.
- Built and organised the Jira backlog.
- Planned four one-week Scrum sprints.
- Used story points to estimate Agile delivery effort.
- Documented sprint reviews and retrospectives.
- Created final delivery documentation for portfolio evidence.

## Business Analysis Value

This project demonstrates how business analysis and Agile delivery practices can be applied to an analytics dashboard project. Instead of only showing the final dashboard, the project shows how requirements are gathered, work is structured, delivery is planned and progress is tracked through Jira.

## Final Outcome

The project successfully demonstrates an end-to-end Agile delivery workflow for a SaaS churn dashboard. It shows the ability to translate a business problem into requirements, structure delivery using Scrum, manage a Jira backlog and document sprint-based progress from planning to final delivery.
