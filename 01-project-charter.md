# Agile Analytics Delivery Project: SaaS Churn Dashboard

## Project Type
Simulated Agile delivery case study

## Project Goal
To plan and manage the Agile delivery of a SaaS customer churn and revenue-at-risk dashboard using Scrum and Jira. The project simulates how a Business Analyst would gather requirements, define user stories, manage a product backlog, plan sprints and support delivery of an analytics dashboard for stakeholder decision-making.

## Business Problem
A SaaS business needs to understand which customer segments are most likely to churn, how much recurring revenue is at risk and where retention efforts should be prioritised. The dashboard is intended to help Customer Success, Revenue Operations and senior stakeholders monitor churn drivers, revenue exposure and retention priorities in one executive view.

## Target Stakeholders
- Head of Customer Success
- Revenue Operations Manager
- Product Manager
- Executive Leadership Team
- Data / BI Team

## My Role
Business Analyst / Agile Delivery Analyst

## Responsibilities
- Gather and structure stakeholder requirements
- Define epics, user stories and acceptance criteria
- Build and prioritise the product backlog
- Plan sprint delivery using Scrum
- Set up and manage a Jira Scrum board
- Track delivery progress across analysis, dashboarding and review tasks
- Document sprint reviews, retrospective notes and final delivery summary

## Final Output
An Agile delivery case study including:
- Project charter
- Stakeholder requirements
- Product backlog
- Epics and user stories
- Acceptance criteria
- Sprint plan
- Jira Scrum board screenshots
- Sprint review notes
- Retrospective summary
- Final delivery summary
