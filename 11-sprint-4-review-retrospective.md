## Sprint Review

During Sprint 4, the dashboard outputs were reviewed to ensure that churn, revenue-risk and filtering features were aligned with the project requirements. The sprint also focused on turning the analysis into clear business recommendations for stakeholder decision-making.

The final recommendations highlighted where customer retention efforts should be prioritised based on churn drivers, customer segments and revenue exposure.

## Sprint Outcome

Sprint 4 was completed successfully. All 3 planned stories were moved to Done in Jira, completing 8 out of 8 planned story points.

## What Went Well

- Dashboard outputs were reviewed against the original requirements.
- Final recommendations were written in business language.
- The project showed a complete Agile delivery flow from requirements to final delivery.
- Jira evidence clearly demonstrated sprint planning and delivery progress.

## What Could Be Improved

- More stakeholder feedback could be added before final delivery.
- Future versions could include dashboard usability testing.
- Acceptance criteria could be reviewed formally at the end of every sprint.

## Lessons Learned

This sprint showed the importance of validating outputs before final delivery. A dashboard is only useful when the metrics are accurate, the story is clear and the recommendations support real business decisions.
