# Sprint 1 Review and Retrospective

## Sprint Name

Sprint 1: Requirements

## Sprint Goal

Define stakeholder requirements, confirm dashboard success criteria and prepare the Agile delivery plan for the SaaS churn dashboard.

## Sprint Dates

3 July to 10 July

## Sprint Scope

Sprint 1 focused on requirements discovery and success criteria definition for the SaaS churn dashboard project.

## Completed Work Items

| Jira ID | Story | Status | Story Points |
|---|---|---|---:|
| SCRUM-8 | Define stakeholder requirements | Done | 3 |
| SCRUM-9 | Define dashboard success criteria | Done | 3 |

## Sprint Review

During Sprint 1, the key business stakeholders were identified and their dashboard requirements were documented. The project scope was clarified around churn visibility, revenue-risk quantification and business recommendations.

The sprint also defined the success criteria for the dashboard, including clear KPI visibility, segment-level churn analysis, revenue-at-risk reporting and stakeholder-friendly recommendations.

## Sprint Outcome

Sprint 1 was completed successfully. Both planned stories were moved to Done in Jira, completing 6 out of 6 planned story points.

## What Went Well

- Stakeholder groups were clearly identified.
- Dashboard objectives were translated into business requirements.
- Success criteria were defined before analysis and dashboard development.
- Jira epics, stories and sprint planning created a structured Agile delivery process.

## What Could Be Improved

- Future sprints should include more detailed task breakdowns under each story.
- Dashboard requirements could be supported with a simple wireframe before development.
- Acceptance criteria should be reviewed again before final dashboard validation.

## Lessons Learned

This sprint showed the importance of defining stakeholder needs before starting analysis. Clear requirements help prevent dashboard scope creep and make the final output more useful for business decision-making.
